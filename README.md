# Coels Thing

## Overview
Coels Thing is an automated CRM orchestration platform that centralizes customer communication and sales data across HubSpot, Stripe, and Google Sheets. It automatically categorizes emails, syncs payment data, and provides a unified dashboard to eliminate manual follow-up work for busy executives.

## Problem
Customer emails are scattered and require manual categorization, payment data doesn't flow into CRM automatically, and there's no single view of contact activity across systems, forcing the founder to work IN the business instead of ON it

## Target User
CEO/founder of a $10M+ marketing services company who needs to automate customer communication workflows and focus on high-level selling rather than manual CRM tasks

## Features
- Automated email categorization and tagging based on HubSpot contacts
- Stripe payment data sync with HubSpot revenue tracking
- Google Sheets bi-directional contact sync with backup exports
- Unified dashboard showing cross-platform contact activity
- Automatic contact creation from Stripe customer data
